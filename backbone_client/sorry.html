January 15th 2014 v1.90

After 40 weeks as an offline Chrome app LingSync v1.40 has became available outside of the Chrome Store, online at http://app.lingsync.org. In November 2012, a significant change in Chrome Apps for Chrome 24 meant that we could no longer use our app offline without significant modifications. That day has come. In December 2013 we were forced to convert this app into a Chrome Extension (icon in the Chrome menu bar). In preparation, as of version v1.72 much of the corpus settings began moving into the corpus' pages themselves. As of version v.190 Chrome will no longer let you use the app as Chrome app, instead IF you are used to using the Prototype app and you would like to continue using it, we have made a "hidden" link on the Corpus Pages, the video below shows you how to access it. We are encouraging all new users to use the LingSync Online app, which has a much more streamlined layout for data entry. Both apps are updated every two weeks.
