January 28th 2012

After 40 weeks as a Chrome app, v1.40 of LingSync now available outside of the Chrome Store online at LingSync.org and soon on Android. A change in Chrome Apps for Chrome 24 means our testing app can no longer contact our testing databases. We are working on a workaround or a re-infrastructuring. We hope to have the workaround ready in a few weeks depending on how busy we are this semester. For now you can use the online version, or the stable version if you want to be offline (note the stable version is currently not very good at multi-user corpora).

If you want more details or updates you can leave comments/your vote on our issue #828 or complain on the thread we created the Chrome Extension Developer's Google Group. It turns out we aren't the only app that has been put in limbo by the transition from legacy apps to packaged apps. Packaged apps are pretty awesome...they look like a normal desktop app. We can't wait until they are available for us to transition to...
