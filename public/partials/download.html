Use LingSync
There are several ways to use LingSync.

Apps

LingSync
The fully-featured version of LingSync is offered as a Google Chrome Extension. This version includes advanced search and data manipulation, as well as offline capability and more!
LingSync Lite
The lite version of LingSync is a 100% web-based app that allows for quick and easy entry of your data. Currently in beta.
Go to LingSync Lite
LingSync for Android
The mobile version of LingSync gives you access to the app anytime, anywhere on your Android device.
